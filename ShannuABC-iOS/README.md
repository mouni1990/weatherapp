# ShannuABC iOS

Kids learning app for alphabets, numbers, tracing, and puzzles — wrapped as a native iOS app.

## How to open in Xcode

1. Open **ShannuABC-iOS.xcodeproj** in Xcode (double-click or `open ShannuABC-iOS.xcodeproj`)
2. Select your development team in **Signing & Capabilities**
3. Choose a simulator or device and click **Run** (⌘R)

## Contents

- SwiftUI app with WKWebView
- Bundled web app (index.html, app.js, styles.css) from ShannuABC
- Targets iOS 15+
- Same development team as WeatherApp
